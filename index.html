Welcome!

I am a PhD candidate in Economics at the European University Institute (EUI). My supervisors are Russell Cooper and Giacomo Calzolari.

My research agenda encompasses two primary areas:

  1. The Interplay Between Competition and Labour Markets, which explores wage bargaining and competition, and the impact of labour market institutions on firm dynamics in imperfectly competitive markets.
  2. Industrial Policy, focusing on the effects of state aid on firm performance and worker outcomes, as well as the interplay between industrial policies, labour market frictions, and institutional frameworks.
